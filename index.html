About Me — The Beauty Reaction Behind Your Brand

I’m Marija — a chemist by education, a storyteller by calling, and the quiet force behind brand narratives that feel intimate, intelligent, and unmistakably refined.

My world lives between two stages: the laboratory, where I explore the invisible logic of molecules, and the story, where I translate that logic into emotions people can’t quite explain but can’t forget either.

That duality is the core of The Beauty Reaction — the moment when science, story, and brand experience align so seamlessly that people feel your product before they ever try it.

With a Master’s in Organic Chemistry and ongoing PhD research, I bring scientific authority to beauty brands that value depth, credibility, and well-grounded formulation storytelling.

With over 15 years of acting experience, I give those same brands presence, emotion, and timing — the kind of narrative rhythm that stays under the skin.

I don’t create “content.” I craft luxury storytelling and strategic brand writing designed to shape how your beauty brand — skincare, makeup, haircare, fragrance, clinical, or luxury — is felt.

Science is my structure. Story is my language. Luxury is the way it all comes together.

If you want your brand to sound powerful, gentle, luxurious, scientific, convincing — or all of that at once — you’re in the right place.

I don’t just write texts. I shape perception. I design emotion. I build stories beauty consumers remember.
